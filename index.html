About Aevor

Founded in 2020, Aevor embodies the spirit of Swiss horology with a fresh perspective. We blend traditional watchmaking techniques with contemporary aesthetics, creating pieces that stand the test of time. Our brand is dedicated to young professionals who appreciate the artistry of precision engineering and the allure of minimalist design.

Heritage: Inspired by centuries of horological mastery, Aevor watches are crafted to honor the legacy of fine timepieces while embracing innovation.
